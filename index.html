The platform

Three things.
One platform.

Spliq is not a bill-splitting app. It is a group finance platform built around the three things groups actually need.

Discover

Set your budget and headcount. Spliq surfaces restaurants, hotels, activities, and experiences your group can actually afford — ranked by fit, rating, and proximity.

Plan

Create a group, define the goal, set the split. Whether it's a baecation, a team dinner, or a gift collection — Spliq brings your people and their money into one place.

Spliq

Everyone pays their share upfront into an escrow wallet. When everyone's in, money goes straight to the merchant. Leftovers come back to every wallet automatically.

How it works

From idea to settled
in four steps.

1
Create a goal
Name your goal — a trip, a dinner, a gift, a party fund, anything. Set the total amount and how you want to split it. Add your people by username or email.
2
Everyone contributes
Each member gets a push notification with their share. They fund from their personal wallet in one tap. The group ledger updates in real time for everyone to see.
3
Pay the merchant directly
Once fully funded, the host triggers payment. Funds go straight from the group wallet to the merchant's bank account. No cash. No middleman. No risk.
4
Settle and refund
Bill settled? Any excess is calculated and returned proportionally to every contributor's wallet automatically. Full breakdown sent to the group.

Features

Everything your group
needs. Nothing it doesn't.

Escrow-first payments

Funds are held safely in a group wallet until everyone has contributed. Payment to the merchant only happens when the group wallet is 100% funded. No partial payments. No IOUs.

Full financial transparency

Every group member sees the real-time ledger — who has paid, who hasn't, the exact amount in the wallet, and the full breakdown when the bill is settled. No more 'did you send it?'

Pay For Me

Any member can request a sponsor for their share. The sponsor funds it, it's recorded transparently on the ledger for all to see, and the group moves on. Perfect for dates, surprises, and gestures.

Automatic refunds

If the final bill comes in under what was collected, the excess is calculated and returned proportionally to every contributor's personal wallet instantly. No one needs to ask.

Reusable groups

A group you create for tonight's dinner becomes the group for next month's trip and the Christmas gift collection. No rebuilding. The group is the permanent financial home for that social circle.

Direct to merchant

Funds go straight from the group wallet to the restaurant's or vendor's bank account. The host never handles cash. There is no middleman. Full accountability from first contribution to final payment.

Individual extras

If one member orders something additional outside the agreed group bill, it's tracked as a separate tab. The group bill is not affected. The final payment to the merchant remains a single clean transaction.

Budget-aware discovery

Enter your total budget and headcount. Spliq filters and ranks venues, restaurants, and experiences that actually fit. No more choosing a place and then discovering it's out of reach.
